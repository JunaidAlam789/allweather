About US

All Weather Construction is a registered Civil, Electrical, Telecom, Health & Safety and Environmental Engineering Services Company. The firm is owned by professional engineer, with diverse experience in civil, electrical, telcom, Prefab, HSE and environmental engineering works. All Weather Construction is registered with Pakistan Engineering Council in Category C-5.

Being a construction company, we are trying to provide best possible services to our clients with highest quality standards. All Weather Construction is committed to play its positive role in building up a society where peace, equality and justice are the most practiced features.
